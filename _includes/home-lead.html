Community in Unity: Building Resilience to Trauma invites you to a convening of community and family serving organizations and professionals to develop tools to build resilience in the face of trauma.

This convening will focus on the latest research on the impact of trauma, explore how communities can respond to trauma and build resilience, and share resources and tools available for organizations and communities. This convening will instill practitioners with hope and a sense of purpose in their work with children, families, and communities that have been affected by trauma.
